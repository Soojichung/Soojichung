A3, THE MUSEUM OF NEW ASIA, AFRICA, CENTRAL & LATIN AMERICA
2019, Brand Identity, Concept Design, Poster Design, and Exhibition Catalog

To create a dynamic visual identity for a gallery or museum.

As a group, we decided to create a hypothetical museum. We created a service experience design for a cultural sector that focuses on cultural representation in Asia, Africa, Central & Latin America that is currently non-existent in London as a means to educate and inform. The desired supportive environment which allows visitors to experience a personal reflection and heuristic self-discourse; to "travel, without traveling".

Project team: Farida Eltigi, Jinhye Park and Kimberly Chuang
