Hoo(後)
2019,Photography, Print, Photo Book Design

Making an exciting visually led mini photo book based on the given theme; A call to adventure.
It is an 8 Page mini photo book using the ideas of journey and adventure.

Studying abroad has not been easy. It is a journey of self-discovery, especially food. Everything needs to be decided by myself. Buying the groceries, menu decisions, and etc. I am always looking for what to eat. My adventure was to record what I had for each meal and created interesting collage with it.
