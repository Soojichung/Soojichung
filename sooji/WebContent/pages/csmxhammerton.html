CSM Beer Identity Project 2020 (CSM X Hammerton Brewery)
2020, Visual Identity, Identity, and Can Design

Awarded the runner up prize in the CSM Beer Identity Project 2020 (GCD x CSM Platform Bar x Hammerton Brewery)

Hammerton Brewery is a microbrewery based in Islington, London. This project was a collaboration with CSM students and local suppliers.
Created designs to be wrapped onto the tin, giving more flexibility to create small batch beer with its own identity.
The idea was my personal experience to explore a new variety of learning after moving from Fine Art to Graphics.
Afterwards, my ‘DO IT’ design was selected by Hammerton Brewery Company and I participated in designing their new can design.
