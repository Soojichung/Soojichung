TICKER
2021, Application Design, Identity, and Web Design

Ticker is a beauty camera application with beautification functions based on AR technology. With Ticker, you can instantly take perfect selfies and touch-up the photos with our photo editor. It contains a variety of functions including, photo editing tools, make-up styles, video calls while make-ups on. Ticker is an integrated beauty platform developed to access various communication activities. The most significant difference from the existing beauty camera app is the E-commerce function that allows you to purchase cosmetic brands instantly after experiencing them through AR beauty camera. As a team, we collaboratively worked on developing the server and designing the UX/UI design of this beauty camera application, Ticker.
